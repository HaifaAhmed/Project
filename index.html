About

MEET THE ADventure

Girls!

The Adventure Girls met during a coding course. As they learnt how to code they began to plot their global travels. The adventure girls was born, as was this blog as they traveled the world in search of adventure. Sarah, Haifa and Katherine each have their own personal interests. Sarah is the thrill seeker and loves all action activities. Haifa is all up for local culture & arts and Katherine loves to discover local nature spots as well exploring the local cuisines!

We hope you enjoy this blog and we hope there is somehting for everyone's interests. We would like to visit as many destinations as we can over the next two years and would love to share our journey with you! Don't forget to subscribe!.
